Pectorals

Commonly occur due to overstretching, pressure, or repetitive motion, especially in athletes.

Activities like tennis, golf, gymnastics, and weightlifting can lead to strains.

Ankle Pain Relief Exercises:

  • Pectoralis Major Stretch: Stand upright with feet shoulder-width apart. Place forearm and palm flat against a sturdy vertical surface like a wall at shoulder height. Rotate body away from extended arm to stretch chest muscles. Repeat on both sides.
  • Doorway Stretch: Stand in a doorway with feet together. Bend elbows at a 90-degree angle and place forearms on doorframe at shoulder height. Take a small step forward, leaning into the doorway for a comfortable chest stretch. Maintain a neutral spine. Repeat on both sides.
  • Chest Opener Stretch: Sit or stand with a straight spine and relaxed shoulders. Interlace fingers behind back, palms facing inward. Lift arms straight while keeping the chest open. Feel the stretch in the chest and shoulders. Keep the neck and jaw relaxed. Repeat as directed.
