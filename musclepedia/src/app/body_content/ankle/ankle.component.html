Ankle Pain

Ankle pain encompasses discomfort affecting any part of the ankle.

Commonly alleviated with rest, ice, and over-the-counter pain medication.

Ankle Pain Relief Exercises:

  • Ankle Stretch: Use a Sportcord over the foot's instep, extend the leg, and hold for five seconds. Repeat twenty times for each foot.
  • Ankle Flexion: Sit with one leg extended, flex the top of the toe backward, and hold for twenty seconds. Repeat ten times for each leg.
  • Heel Raises: Stand near a wall or chair, raise one foot backward until the lower leg forms a 90-degree angle. Hold for five seconds, repeat ten times for each leg.
  • Heel Slides: Lie on the floor, slide one heel towards the buttocks as far as comfortable, then return to the starting position. Repeat ten times for each leg.
  • Ankle Rotations: Raise the foot about 20 inches off the floor, rotate it to the left and then to the right with the toe upward. Hold for ten seconds, repeat twenty times for each leg.
