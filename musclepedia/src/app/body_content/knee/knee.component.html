Knee Pain

Knee pain is discomfort in or around the knee joint, often resulting from injuries or irritation.

Knee joint connects thigh bone (femur) to shin bone (tibia), supporting body weight during movement.

Knee Pain Relief Exercises:

  • Overuse: Repetitive motions from physical activities or sports.
  • Arthritis: Inflammation and pain in joints, including knee.
  • Injuries: Traumas, falls, or sports-related incidents causing damage.
  • Common injuries include sprains, ligament tears, meniscus tears, fractures, and dislocations.
