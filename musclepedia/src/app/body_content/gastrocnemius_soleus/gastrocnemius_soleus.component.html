Gastrocnemius and Soleus

Often caused by mild injuries like strains or cramps, but severe or persistent pain may indicate inadequate blood flow.

RICE method: Rest, ice, compression, and elevation.

Gentle stretching may help alleviate soreness or cramps.

Ankle Pain Relief Exercises:

  • Towel Stretch: Sit on a hard surface with one leg stretched out. Loop a towel around toes and ball of foot, pull toward body with knee straight. Hold for 15-30 seconds, repeat 3 times.
  • Standing Calf Stretch: Face a wall, hands at eye level, one leg back with heel on floor, other leg forward. Lean into wall with back foot slightly inward, feel stretch in calf. Hold for 15-30 seconds, switch legs, repeat 3 times each.
  • Resisted Ankle Plantar Flexion: Sit with leg outstretched, loop tubing around ball of foot. Hold tubing ends, press ball of foot down, point toes, stretch tubing. Do 3 sets of 10.
  • Heel Raise: Stand behind chair, raise onto toes, hold 5 seconds, lower slowly. Repeat 10 times, do 3 sets. Progress to one leg only.
  • Single Leg Balance: Stand unsupported, balance on one leg. Start with eyes open, progress to eyes closed. Hold for 30 seconds, repeat 3 times. Try standing on a pillow for added challenge.
